Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add the bot to my server?

Click the invite button and select your server.

Is the bot free?

Yes! The bot is completely free to use.

What permissions does the bot need?

It needs permission to connect, speak in voice channels, and view text channels.

How do I play music?

Use /play followed by a song name or YouTube URL.

How can I skip the current song?

Use /skip to jump to the next track in the queue.

How does the queue work?

Use /queue to see all songs. Use /playnext to prioritize a song.

What if the bot doesn’t leave the voice channel?

The bot disconnects when music ends or no one is left in the channel.

Does the bot support playlists?

Currently it supports individual songs. Playlist support is planned soon.
